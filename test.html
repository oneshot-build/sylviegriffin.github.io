About Sylvie

From Corporate Success to Aligned Living

I understand what it's like to have everything society says you should want—and still feel unsatisfied with your life. For years, I climbed the wrong ladder, following the road society had already drawn for me.

I am Belgian-Brazilian and studied Economics in Belgium. I started my career in the consulting industry and then worked for 10+ years at the Belgian and European Central Banks.   For all those years I knew that something was missing but the life I was dreaming of seemed out of reach.

As a result of this misalignment I suffered from many health issues. Conventional medicine could never help me much so I studied and tested all kinds of alternative medicines including Psychotherapy, Ayurveda, Chinese medicine, Energy healing and Neurosciences.   My transformation began when I started listening to what my heart and body were telling me. trapped emotions and the stress caused by an unaligned life were the root cause of my health conditions.

I left my corporate career, trained as a life coach, yoga teacher, and reiki practitioner, and moved to Brazil to follow my dreams and create a life that truly aligned with my soul's deepest desires.

Now I help people reconnect with their true nature and discover their unique mission on earth. I believe we all have a specific purpose, and when we find it and serve it, abundance flows naturally. Your dreams and what brings you joy aren't distractions—they're signposts pointing you toward your true calling.

Through coaching, I guide you in peeling back layers of societal conditioning, reconnecting with your authentic self, and creating a life that feels deeply aligned and fulfilling.
