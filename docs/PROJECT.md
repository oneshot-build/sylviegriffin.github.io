# Sylvie Griffin - Life Coach Website

## Project Overview

This is the personal website for Sylvie Griffin, a Belgian-Brazilian life coach, yoga teacher, and reiki practitioner based in Brazil serving clients worldwide.

## About Sylvie

Sylvie is a certified life coach, yoga teacher, and reiki master practitioner who transitioned from a successful corporate career in economics and banking to follow her passion for helping others align with their true purpose.

### Background
- Former Banking Executive & Economist
- Studied Economics in Belgium
- 10+ years experience at Belgian and European Central Banks
- Certified Life Coach & Yoga Teacher
- Reiki Master Practitioner
- Now based in Brazil, serving clients worldwide

### Mission
Sylvie helps people reconnect with their true nature and discover their unique mission on earth. She believes we all have a specific purpose, and when we find it and serve it, abundance flows naturally.
