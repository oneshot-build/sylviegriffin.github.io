# Sylvie Griffin - Life Coach Website

## Project Overview
Professional website for Sylvie Griffin, a certified life coach, yoga teacher, and reiki practitioner. The site showcases her transformational coaching services and provides an easy way for potential clients to book sessions.

## Purpose
- Present Sylvie's coaching philosophy and approach
- Share her personal transformation story (from corporate banking to aligned living)
- Showcase available services (Life Coaching, Yoga & Movement, Conscious Travel, Corporate events)
- Enable easy booking through WhatsApp integration
- Build trust through professional credentials and authentic storytelling

## Target Audience
- Professionals feeling unfulfilled in their careers
- Individuals seeking life purpose and direction
- People interested in holistic wellness approaches
- Corporate groups seeking wellness programs
- Anyone drawn to conscious living and personal transformation

## Key Features
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for all devices
- **Professional Branding**: Clean, wellness-focused aesthetic
- **WhatsApp Integration**: Direct booking through WhatsApp
- **Service Showcase**: Detailed information about coaching offerings
- **Personal Story**: Authentic narrative of transformation
- **Credentials Display**: Professional qualifications and certifications

## Technical Stack
- **Framework**: Astro v5.18.1 (Static Site Generator)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS with custom design system
- **Integrations**: React components for interactive elements
- **Hosting**: Static site deployment ready
- **Performance**: Optimized images, minimal JavaScript

## Services Offered
1. **Life Coaching**: Purpose discovery and aligned living guidance
2. **Yoga & Movement**: Physical and spiritual wellness practices  
3. **Conscious Travel**: Transformational travel experiences
4. **Corporate Events & Groups**: Group coaching and yoga sessions
